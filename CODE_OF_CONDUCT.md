# Code of Conduct

We want a constructive, technically rigorous community. Treat contributors,
users, and reviewers with respect. Harassment, discrimination, threats,
personal attacks, doxxing, and sharing another person's confidential material
are not acceptable.

When reviewing automation or safety behavior, challenge evidence and design
choices rather than people. Report conduct concerns privately through the
repository's security or maintainer contact channel. Maintainers may remove,
edit, or reject contributions that violate this standard.
